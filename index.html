Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the MindStreak - Daily Mood Tracker app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Berkcan Yüzer (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed with your privacy in mind. All your personal data (mood entries, activity logs, notes, streaks, scores, and settings) is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. The Service Provider does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

The Application does not collect any of the following information:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Your browsing behavior or usage analytics
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond what you voluntarily enter into the app

Third-Party Advertising: The Application displays ads through Google AdMob. While the Application itself does not collect device information, AdMob may collect certain device and usage information for ad delivery purposes. This is handled entirely by Google's advertising services and subject to their privacy policy (see Third Party Access section below).

Local Notifications: The Application uses local notifications to remind you to log your daily mood. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external data collection or transmission.

Third Party Access

The Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are the links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
  • Google Privacy Policy - general Google services privacy information

Personalized Ads: The Application includes an optional setting for personalized advertising. When enabled (default), AdMob may use your advertising ID and browsing behavior to show relevant ads, which can increase ad revenue by 30-50%. You can disable personalized ads at any time in the app's Settings screen, which will request non-personalized ads instead.

The Service Provider does not share, sell, or transmit any of your personal mood data, notes, or activity logs to any third parties. Your data stays exclusively on your device.

The Service Provider may disclose information only:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  • when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the Google Play Store.

Ad Preferences: You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by:

  • Opening the Application's Settings screen
  • Toggling off "Personalized Ads" under the Ad Preferences section
  • Or by adjusting your device's ad tracking settings (Android: Google Settings > Ads)
Data Retention Policy

All your data is stored locally on your device. The Service Provider does not have access to your data and therefore has nothing to delete on their end.

When you uninstall the Application, all your locally stored data (mood entries, activities, notes, streaks, scores, and settings) will be permanently deleted from your device. There are no backups stored on external servers.

If you have any questions about your data, please contact the Service Provider at berkcanyuzer@gmail.com.

Children

The Service Provider does not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. In the case the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal information, the Service Provider will immediately take necessary actions to remove such information.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information through the Application, please contact the Service Provider at berkcanyuzer@gmail.com so that appropriate actions can be taken.

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Since all your data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to external servers, your data security primarily depends on your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

The Service Provider recommends:

  • Using a secure screen lock on your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals
  • Being cautious when granting app permissions

The Application requests only necessary permissions (notifications for reminders) and does not request access to sensitive data like contacts, camera, microphone, or location.
